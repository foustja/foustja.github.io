Luxaeterna


Luxaterna is a light sculpture made with addressable WS2822S LEDs embedded in pentagonal PCBs (i.e., BlinkyTiles) and controlled with an Adafruit Metro Mini. Arduino/C++ and FastLED allow cycling through a series of patterns and colors. (Sadly BlinkyTiles no longer appear to be available, but you can learn more about them and their uses here.)

Pre-recorded (Full Screen)

Inspiration for Luxaeterna was provided by Bunnie Huang's masterpiece Polyhedrone (which I had the pleasure of seeing in Black Rock City in 2017), and Solcrusher (seen at Luminata at Green Lake Park in Seattle several years ago).
