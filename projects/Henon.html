Henon

The Henon map displays chaotic behavior. You can learn more about it here.

The following formulas are iterated, and the orbit generated is plotted in the xy plane:
xn+1=1-axn2+yn
yn+1=bxn

To obtain the rich and complex behavior shown in this animation, parameter b is held steady at -0.9998, and parameter a varies incrementally between -0.6 and 0.9.
